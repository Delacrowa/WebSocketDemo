Goal: Run around and hit people to increase your score and try to stay #1
Controls:
WASD to move
Space to dodge (beginning you are invulnerable when you are glowing white)
Pick Up Item/Respawn: E
Switch Weapon: Left Shift
Aim attacks with mouse
Basic attack: Left click
Alternate attack: right click
Special attack: middle click
Tips:
- There are weapons on the ground you can pick up.
- Killing enemies will drop their weapons.
- You start with a basic sword, and can pick up a spear or a great sword.
- Also the higher your score, the more LIFE STEAL you get, you will gain health when you damage enemies.
- You can swap weapons instantly, use this to trick opponents.
- When you are in a "get hurt" animation, you can dodge out of it early so don't get comboed!
- You can also dodge to cancel the item pick up and respawn animations
- Dealing damage increases your score based on the score of the enemy. Dealing the killing blow gives a lot of points.
- Full screen browser so entire screen fits so that scroll wheel doesn't activate on middle click.
